# E-Commerce Sales & Profitability Dashboard (Advanced Excel)

## 📌 Project Overview
An interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze over 9,000 retail transactions from a multi-regional e-commerce marketplace. The goal of this analysis was to move beyond high-level sales volumes and isolate structural operational bottlenecks impacting the company's net profit margins.

## 🔍 Key Business Insights Discovered
* **Critical Profit Drain:** The **Tables** sub-category acts as a major bottleneck—generating high revenue (₹2.06L) but resulting in a severe net loss of **₹17,725** due to aggressive promotional markdown discounts exceeding 20%.
* **High-Efficiency Drivers:** **Copiers** yield the maximum net profit margins despite a lower overall transactional volume, making them prime candidates for focused marketing campaigns.
* **Geographic Anchors:** Market performance is heavily centralized, with **California** and **New York** generating over 50% of total global profitability.

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations
1. Enforce a strict **15% promotional discount cap** on all Furniture and Table categories to immediately protect operational margins.
2. Reallocate marketing budgets away from underperforming discount cycles and shift promotional visibility toward high-margin items like Copiers and Technology accessories.
